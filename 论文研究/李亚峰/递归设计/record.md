## Theorem 3.1

Proof:
* Given:
    * $d = \frac{4n}{2n+1}$
    * Lyapunov function$V_{1}(x_{1}) = \frac{x_{1}^{2}}{2}$

Using (3.1)
$$
\dot{V}_{1}(x_{1}) \le x_{1}x_{2} + x_{1}^{2}\gamma_{1}(x_{1})\\
\le x_{1}(x_{2}-x_{2}^{*}) + x_{1}x_{2}^{*} + x_{1}^{d}\tilde{\rho_{1}(x_{1})}
$$
